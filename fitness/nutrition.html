Do not approach your nutrition plan as a diet plan, but rather, a lifestyle change. If you ake a mermanent chnage, you wull see big results.

What to Eat

Use the following as a guideline:

  • Protien, such as eggs, chicken, and lean red meat
  • Vegetables, but avoid corn and peas
  • Fruits
  • Fat, such as olive oil, nuts, and seeds

Limit your consumption of bread, pasta, white potatoes, and processed food. There foods are high in carbs.

When you shop for groceries, shop outside asiles and buy organic food when possible. Stock up on fresh vegetables, almond flour, and flax seed.

Meal of the Week

Herb Roasted Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breast (organic preferred)
  • 1 tsp herbes de provence
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper

Instructions:

Combine all ingredients in a plastic bag and marinate for at least an hour. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Bake chicken for 30-35 min or until juices run clear. Then broil.
